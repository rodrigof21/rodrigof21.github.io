Website for a Charity Institution

In our final year of High School me and two colleagues of mine decided to participate in a contest for website building. We discovered that there was an institution in our hometown that didn't have a website. After a meeting with them, we agreed to build them a website for free, that we would also use on the contest. The video bellow was made for said contest and it shows how it looked after the two months of hard work. We ended up in second place nationally. The website has since been updated and maintained by a professional team of developers.
